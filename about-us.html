About Us

Who We Are?

We believe that 'Likes' and 'Smiles' can be converted to business. The number of thumbs up we are getting from our customers for the last 2 decades proves it. Since its installation in 1990, Diwaniya Furnishings grew as one of the best in the total home furnishing segment. Diwaniya Furnishings is the part of a large business conglomerate with diversified business interests in Home and Interior Decoration, Retail stores, Health Care, Real Estate Development, Agri and Aquaculture. Diwaniya exhibits the modish designs of both Indian and imported Furnishing Fabrics, Curtains, Drapery Rods, all types of Blinds, Furniture, Kitchen Cabinets, Mattresses, Pillows, Bed linen, Wooden floorings, and lots of other interior accessories. Our flagship showroom at Palarivattom, Cochin; is equipped with sophisticated machineries and tools for providing the most unique interior decoration.

The CEO, Mr. Imam Salih's experience in a middle east company about soft furnishings and interior decoration ,paved the idea of starting this unequalled venture in 1990 and since then,we have beautified scores of homes, hotels and offices. Expansion started in 1993 by being the distributor for M/s Sintex Industries Ltd and opening branches at Trivandrum and at Kollam. By 2005, we opened our second branch at Kadavanthara,Cochin. In November 2005, our present flagship store at own premises started functioning. Presently, Diwaniya operates from its 10,000 sqft own multilevel showroom at Palarivattom and with another Storage, workshop and production/finishing area of 20,000 sqft. Diwaniya is equipped with a furnishing team experienced in international standards. Moreover,We have a professionally qualified team which overlooks the administration and sales along with technically skilled, gulf experienced work force.
