Services

Curtain Making & Fixing

Our in-house designers/experts will visit your place and give you free advice on the best options available to beautify your interiors/windows. Our 10000 sqft showroom features the finest fabrics of latest trends and designs, both Indian & Imported at the best prices in the region ranging from Rs 40-1200; along with a display of various fascinating patterns at our display apartments. You can also refer to the latest catalogues available from across the globe. Besides, there is an array of different types of Drapery Rods and accessories like Tiebacks, Tieback holders, Tassels etc to add more life and charm to your window dressings. After taking measurements, we will provide you with a detailed estimate according to your budget and supports you in selecting fabrics, designs and stitching patterns that will blend with your interiors. Once this is through, then just sit back and relax while we wove the enchanting furnishing threads into your home within the lowest possible time frame. We are having the state-of the art machines with gulf trained staff and imported accessories to give a perfect and lasting finishes to the drapes which no one else have in this region at present.

Painting & Polishing

We take up interior and exterior wall painting using the latest trends and colours. Furniture painting and polishing is another avenue in which we can produce excellent results.

Made to Order Furnishing Accessories

We undertake making of Quilts, Bedcovers, throw/scatter Cushions, Futons, Futon covers, Table cloth and mats for homes, hotels and Corporates as per specific designs.

Curtain Dry cleaning & Reinstalling

We are keen in the follow-up services to our customers. We can remove your soiled Curtains, get them Dry washed and ironed properly, put back while checking the rods for any hooks and runners to be replaced. All well within nominal charges.

Landscaping & Gardening

Interior and exterior Gardening is an inevitable part of modern living. We shall provide you with designs and estimates. Give you the entire solutions of installing and maintenance also. We are having a good collection of imported artificial plants & flowers in our showroom.

Carpet Cleaning & Shampooing

It is difficult to maintain carpets in our climate. We offer you the unique services of cleaning and shampooing along with stain removing to the possible extent; that too at your premises itself, if required.

Express Total Home Furnishing Within 48 hrs

For the urgent and needy customers who want to move in immediately, we provide all readymade furnishing solutions from 12 to 48 hrs time

House keeping and Home Care

This is a unique service of ours which is being admired by everyone now. Especially for the NRIs we take care of the housekeeping needs and prepare the home ready for your brief stay at hometown during vacation. We can even arrange additional facilities to our customers like Security, Pest Control etc.

Mosquito Proofing

To protect against the common menace of mosquitoes, we have three different options of putting preventive shutters. The Netlon mesh with Velcro fasteners is the normal one. The same with fiber mesh is the modern. The latest is with imported aluminum rolling system which is functionally the best.

Re-Upholstery & Cushion Covering

Sofas, Cushions and Dining Chairs need to be given a face-lift once a while. We shall sent our workers across, size up your requirements with possible options for remodeling within the limits. Works out estimates for material and labour. Once agreed, just visualize the entire new look to your living room in a day or two.
