About Us

We believe that 'Likes' and 'Smiles' can be converted to business. The number of thumbs up we are getting from our customers for the last 2 decades proves it. Since its installation in 1990, Diwaniya Furnishings grew as one of the best in the total home furnishing segment. Diwaniya Furnishings is the part of a large business conglomerate with diversified business interests in Home and Interior Decoration, Retail stores, Health Care, Real Estate Development, Agri and Aquaculture.

Diwaniya exhibits the modish designs of both Indian and imported Furnishing Fabrics, Curtains, Drapery Rods, all types of Blinds, Furniture, Kitchen Cabinets, Mattresses, Pillows, Bed linen, Wooden floorings, and lots of other interior accessories. Our flagship showroom at Palarivattom, Cochin; is equipped with sophisticated machineries and tools for providing the most unique interior decoration.

The CEO, Mr. Imam Salih's experience in a middle east company about soft furnishings and interior decoration ,paved the idea of starting this unequalled venture in 1990 and since then,we have beautified scores of homes, hotels and offices.
